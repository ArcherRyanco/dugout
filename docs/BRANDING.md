# Dugout Branding Guide

## Logo
- **Primary**: `/public/logo.png` (badge with mascot)
- **Mascot**: Baseball character with D cap, white uniform, clipboard
- **Style**: Friendly, playful youth sports aesthetic

## Colors
Primary palette extracted from logo:
- **Navy Blue**: `#1e3a5f` (border, headings, "DUGOUT" text)
- **Sky Blue**: `#87ceeb` (backgrounds, "TEAM MANAGER" text)
- **Grass Green**: `#4ade80` (accents, success states)
- **White**: `#ffffff` (backgrounds, mascot)
- **Tan/Brown**: `#d4a574` (clipboard, bat - warm accents)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, rounded sans-serif (like logo text)
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar)

## Tone
- Friendly and approachable
- Youth sports focused
- Parent and coach friendly
- Not corporate - feels like little league

## Icons
- `/public/favicon.ico` - Browser tab
- `/public/apple-touch-icon.png` - iOS home screen (180x180)
- `/public/icon-192.png` - Android/PWA (192x192)
- `/public/icon-512.png` - PWA splash (512x512)

## UI Guidelines
- Rounded corners on cards/buttons (8-12px)
- Soft shadows, not harsh
- Sky blue gradients for headers
- Green for CTAs and success
- Navy for important text
- Mascot can appear in empty states and onboarding
